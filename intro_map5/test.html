Test Market Definition

The following map shows the incidence of spinal cord injuries and population of countries being considered for out test market.

Potential Markets

Of countries evaluated, the one with the highest number of potential patients in terms of spinal chord injuries and population is Russia. However the insecurity and lack of due process makes it a difficult place to run a test market. Portugal has the highest incidence of spinal cord injuries but a much smaller population. Greece was also considered as it has the second highest spinal chord injury incidence and limited health care services for its inhabitants. Finally, highly developed countries like Germany, Iceland, Norway or Denmark have very low incidences of spinal chord injuries, making them inadequate for the kind of trial we are planning.

Conclusion

The country chosen for our test market is Portugal, given their incidence of spinal chord injuries and the ease of obtaining permits and test subjects.
